### **Setting the OctaneRender Engine in Blender Octane Edition**
___
1- Open Blender Octane Edition. Navigate to Edit > Preferences..., and in the Blender Preferences interface, find OctaneRender Engine under the Add-ons tab. If it is not ticked already, tick the checkbox for the OctaneRender option
