## Application:
___
#### **Makeup:**
- First, wear a gown to prevent from staining the models clothes
- With a clean face, moistures the face using a hydrating moisturizer
- Use primer on the whole face. It will help to protect the face, keeps the makeup for long period of time and prevent from sinking into to skin.
- Apply a bit foundation on the lids and powder the lids with a translucent powder
- Use a fluffy brush to apply black eyeshadow on to the lids gradually intensify it.
- blend it out with a clean brush
- Attach false lashes close to the lash line and use a black mascara on both top and bottom lashes.
- Wrap out the fall out if needed
- Apply a little bit of foundation on the jaw line to test if it matches the skin tone. Feel free to mix the foundation to get the prefect shade.
- Start applying the foundation from the center of the face then spread it out using the brush to create natural base. Be careful not to cover the eyebrows with foundation.
- Next, lightly powder the entire face with a translucent powder
- Comb out the eyebrows with a spoolie brush and clear brow gel.
- Contour the underneath the cheeks, nose and a bit on the forehead.
- Highlight the highest point of the face like cheeks, nose bridge, chin,etc.
- Use a nude lipstick and apply lip gloss on top.
- set the face with a dewy setting spray for extra dewy complexion.

#### **Hair:**
- Section out a bit of hair from the front.
- Take rest of the hair and create a bun with grips at the back.
- Take front section,do a middle parting.
- Apply gel on the hair and start creating a waves around the face.
- do the same thing on the other side as well.
